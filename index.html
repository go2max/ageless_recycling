1. How It Works

Welcome to Ageless Recycling! Recycling has never been easier. Here's how we make it simple, rewarding, and environmentally impactful:

  1. Contact Us: Text us at 669-241-6266 to schedule a pickup or ask questions.
  2. Provide Details: Let us know what materials you have and any other relevant information.
  3. Pickup & Record: Our driver will collect your recyclables. Upon request, we can provide detailed information about your past pickups, including amounts and dates.
  4. Track & Benefit: Keep track of your recycling activity and see the impact you’ve made on the environment.

2. Pickup Details

  • Weight Limit: 100 lbs per household per visit
  • Commercial Pickups: Available for small offices and businesses (different rates apply)
  • Out-of-State Items: Not accepted; out-of-state items will be returned
  • Sorting: Clean, sorted recyclables get the best and highest payout

3. Pricing & Payouts

At Ageless Recycling, payouts are based on the cleanliness and sorting of your recyclables. We prioritize pre-sorted materials, while mixed or soiled loads are paid less. Glass must always be separated and NOT included in the overall payment.

Material Type / Load Rate / lb Notes
Pre-sorted load (all acceptable materials except glass) $0.05 Highest payout for clean, sorted items
Mixed load (materials combined) $0.025 Flat rate for combined materials
Soiled or contaminated materials $0.01 May be rejected depending on contamination
Glass- No Paid Value $0.00 Must be separated; pickup only if sorted. Load may be denied at driver discretion if mixed.

Payment Schedule: Payments are posted as soon as possible, but may take up to 2–4 weeks. A minimum balance of $5 is required. Currently, payments are available via PayPal and Venmo.

Bonuses & Loyalty: We love rewarding consistent recycling! Here's how it works:

  • Pickup Bonus: Earn an increased payout of +$0.005/lb for each pickup, as long as you schedule a new pickup within 90 days of your previous one. After 90 days of inactivity, your account returns to the standard payout rate.
  • Referral Credits: Refer a friend and earn $5, but only after your referral completes 5 pickups. The payout occurs on their 5th pickup.

Curious about your potential weekly earnings? Try our interactive Recycling Earnings Calculator to see an estimate.

5. Customer Verification

To ensure secure and accurate recycling pickups, we verify customer details. This helps us protect both your items and your earnings:

6. Driver & Pickup Standards

Our drivers are trained to provide safe, efficient, and professional service, while keeping your recyclables secure:

  • No Cash on Hand: All payments are processed electronically, so drivers do not carry cash.
  • Optimized Routes: Pickups are planned to minimize travel time, reduce emissions, and maintain driver safety.
  • Signature Verification (Optional): For weight and contents confirmation, a signature can be collected to ensure accurate payouts.

  • Professional Conduct: Drivers adhere to courteous, respectful, and reliable service standards at all times.

9. FAQs

Can I recycle large volumes?

Residential pickups are generally limited to 100 lbs per visit. If you regularly have more than this, we’re happy to discuss options to accommodate larger volumes. Commercial pickups can also accept higher amounts at adjusted rates. Contact us to arrange a larger pickup or special scheduling.

Do I have to pay anything?

Nope! You never spend a dime — we pay you for your recyclables. All payouts are digital for safety and convenience via PayPal, Venmo, or Zelle.

What kind of storage containers can I use?

We accept recyclables in any container — bags, boxes, bins, or totes — as long as we can accurately weigh the materials. If you need storage bins or guidance on organizing your recyclables, just let us know and we can provide suggestions. Keeping items sorted by type helps ensure the highest payout.

What if I disagree with my weight or payout?

If you feel the recorded weight or payout doesn’t match your expectations, you can request an on-site verification during pickup. Our driver will re-weigh the materials and confirm totals with a signature if needed, ensuring transparency and fairness for every pickup.

What materials are accepted?

We accept a wide range of recyclables! All items should be clean and sorted for the best payout. Glass must always be separated. Click each category to see details:

Aluminum & Metals

Examples: Soda cans, aluminum foil, clean food tins, small scrap metal

Notes: Rinse all containers; separate aluminum from other metals

Plastic

Examples: Bottles (#1 & #2), jugs, containers, tubs

Notes: Rinse thoroughly; remove caps and labels if possible

Cardboard & Paper

Examples: Corrugated boxes, cereal boxes, newspapers, office paper, magazines

Notes: Flatten boxes; keep free of food or grease contamination

Glass

Examples: Bottles, jars

Notes: Must be separated by color; rinse thoroughly; lids removed

Electronics (E-Waste)

Examples: Small electronics like phones, keyboards, cables

Notes: Check with us first; some electronics require special handling

Beverage Containers (CRV)

Examples: Soda, water, juice bottles, beer cans

Notes: Clean and sorted; aluminum, plastic, and glass separated

Cartons

Examples: Milk cartons, juice cartons, soup cartons

Notes: Rinse before recycling; remove caps

Miscellaneous

Examples: Empty aerosol cans, scrap paperboard, certain plastics (#3-#7 if accepted locally)

Notes: Contact us if unsure; must be clean and safe to handle

Tip: All materials can be collected in any container (bags, boxes, bins, totes) as long as we can accurately weigh them.

What materials are NOT accepted?

To keep our recycling process safe and effective, we cannot accept certain items, including:

  • Out-of-state items (we only handle local California recyclables)
  • Heavily soiled or contaminated materials
  • Non-recyclable items such as hazardous waste or certain electronics

If you’re unsure whether something is acceptable, please contact us before your pickup. Our team is happy to guide you and suggest alternatives.

Can I change my charity choice?

Absolutely! You can update your charity choice anytime via your account settings. Whether you want to try a new cause, support a different sector (animals, veterans, human services, or environment), or just switch things up, it’s simple and flexible. Your future pickups will reflect the updated choice automatically.
